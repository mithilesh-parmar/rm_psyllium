Our commitment

If a lot tests 97.8%, it ships as 98% — at a discount, or it goes back through the mill.

A 99% grade is 99% on paper, on the COA, and in the bag. No headline-purity blending. No "close enough". This is the single rule that has the strongest correlation with long-term contracts in our order book.
