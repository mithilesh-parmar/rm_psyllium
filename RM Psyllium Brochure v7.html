40 Mesh (425 μm) Coarse flaked powder. Retains ≤ 5% on sieve. Optimizes mechanical swelling index in chewable tablets and dry laxative powders.

100 Mesh (150 μm) Fine binder powder. ≥ 95% sieve pass. Replaces gluten structure to restore elasticity and moisture retention in allergen-free baking.
200 Mesh (75 μm) Ultra-fine micro-powder. Instant cold-water hydration. Disperses without grittiness or sedimentation in RTD beverages and drink mixes.

Quality Release & Lot Control Protocol
Strict Lot Isolation Every processed batch is milled, packaged, and isolated in our climate-controlled warehouse. No batch leaves isolation until the full monograph laboratory clearance is officially granted.
Dual COA Verification Shipments are released only when both our in-house laboratory and an independent, accredited third-party NABL laboratory confirm full pharmacopoeial monograph compliance.
24-Month Retain Archiving We retain a 500g sealed control sample of every exported batch for 24 months. This ensures absolute traceability, reference materials for future R&D, and complete customer peace of mind.
100% Non-Blending Policy We never blend marginal husk batches to achieve grade. Off-spec milling runs are immediately and permanently downgraded to animal feed or remilled. Quality is absolute.
