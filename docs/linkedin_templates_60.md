### 📝 Template 9: Viscosity vs. Swelling
#### 📊 Gamma-Ready Slide Outline
```text
Slide 1: Viscosity vs. Swelling Volume in Psyllium Sourcing
- Often confused, but chemically and operationally distinct.
- Both measure water interaction, but they impact different formulations.
- Formulators must understand both to optimize cost and performance.
```
